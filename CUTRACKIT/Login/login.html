About CUTRACKIT

CUTRACKIT is the ultimate platform for Clemson students to connect, organize, and play sports together. Find teammates, create matches, and build your athletic community on campus.

How to Use

  • Find a Game: Browse available matches and join teams looking for players
  • Create a Team: Start your own team and recruit players
  • Join Teams: Find established teams and request to join
  • Leaderboards: Track your stats and see how you rank
