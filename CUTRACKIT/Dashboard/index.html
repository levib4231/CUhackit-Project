Rules

General Conduct

Treat all players, staff, and community members with respect at all times.

Maintain a positive and sportsmanlike attitude during games and interactions.

Harassment, discrimination, or targeted negativity is not tolerated under any circumstances.

Gameplay & Team Rules

Play fairly and avoid any form of cheating, exploiting, or manipulating game systems.

Report suspicious behavior or rule violations to staff immediately.

Stay active and participate consistently if you join a team or event.

Follow your team captain’s instructions and respect team decisions.

Do not join multiple teams in the same bracket unless explicitly allowed.

Event & Participation Rules

Arrive on time for scheduled matches, meetings, or tournaments.

Notify your team or staff if you cannot attend a scheduled event.

Follow all event‑specific rules, including bracket formats and match procedures.

Respect referees, moderators, and event organizers at all times.

Do not attempt to influence or argue with official rulings.
